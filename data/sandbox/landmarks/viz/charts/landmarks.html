An experimental project by Open Data Book Club

PEI, share your quirky landmarks!

Open Data is best explained by an example. Our first example is a list of PEI Landmarks: places we use in everyday conversation. They might exist (Sir John A statue on Queen St, Ch'town, Devil's Punchbowl, near Granville), or might not (the Purple House in East Royalty, the Dairy in Montague)

Add your favourite landmarks to this doc (or on our Facebook page). We'll add them to our list which will be freely available, licensed for re-use, and not a PDF (!)

Thanks so much, PEI ! We're working on adding your suggestions to the map! (from the Open Data Book Club)
